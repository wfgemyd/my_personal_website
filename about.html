A Little About Me

Born in Ukraine, my life's journey embarked on a new direction when my family moved to Israel in 2001. Embracing the vibrant culture and spirit of my new home, I completed high school and embarked on a transformative journey in the Israeli military. Serving three years in the army, particularly in the logistics department of a northern base, I learned not just discipline, but also the art of overcoming challenges through creative problem-solving and the efficient management of large logistics operations. These experiences in the military laid a strong foundation for my abilities in organization and strategic planning.


After my military service, I pursued higher education with a steadfast dedication. At Technion University, Israel, I honed my skills in Practical Engineering, graduating in 2020. My academic journey didn't pause there; it extended to the pursuit of a B.Sc. in Computing at Prague City University. This blend of technical expertise and a contemporary British educational approach equipped me with the tools to lead and navigate complex projects with strategic insight.


My journey also took a pivotal turn during the COVID-19 pandemic, where I embraced the role of a Project Manager. Here, I led significant initiatives to support underprivileged families, orphans, and Holocaust survivors, deepening my commitment to social responsibility. This phase not only refined my leadership and problem-solving skills but also ingrained a profound sense of duty towards community welfare.


Currently residing in Prague, 26 years old, I am an Associate in Management Consulting at KPMG Czech Republic. In this role, I engage with a spectrum of clients, navigating through complex business strategies and operational enhancements. Despite the rigors of a full-time career, my passion for lifelong learning keeps me engaged in advancing my academic qualifications, balancing work with weekend studies.


As I look ahead, I am eager to transition into a more dynamic Project Manager role, leveraging my cumulative knowledge and experience. I aspire to catalyze growth, spearhead innovative projects, and guide teams towards achieving exceptional outcomes. My career trajectory is not just a path of professional growth but a journey marked by a constant pursuit of excellence and a commitment to making a substantial impact in the management landscape.

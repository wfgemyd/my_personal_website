Hobbies

I have a profound passion for data science and electronics, which goes hand in hand with my love for hands-on building. Whether it's creating an innovative 360 camera holder for 3D scanning or conceptualizing a smart house interconnected via Telegram, I thrive on turning complex concepts into tangible solutions.


Martial arts, particularly Jiu-Jitsu and boxing (lets work-out together), are disciplines I deeply enjoy, reflecting my admiration for MMA's multifaceted nature. The focus and discipline I practice in martial arts carry over to my enthusiasm for adrenaline-pumping activities like bike riding, skydiving, and trekking through challenging trails.


I am a lifelong learner, and my mornings are dedicated to expanding my knowledge - starting with the day's news and delving into two or three random facts that catch my interest. This thirst for knowledge also extends to history, especially the history of places I visit. I believe understanding the past enriches my experiences of new locations and brings a depth of appreciation for my surroundings.


Recently, I've discovered a new creative outlet in 3D modeling. It's a skill that combines my technical expertise with an emerging artistic side, allowing me to express my visions in yet another dimension.


Every day is an opportunity to discover and solve; whether it's a daily inconvenience or a larger scale problem, I seek innovative resolutions that streamline and enhance life's experiences.
